How do we make MultiSig a better experience?

Using PSBT (BIP 174), we can interoperate with any other PSBT-supporting wallet or coordinator (such as Sparrow or Specter). By putting QR Codes to use, we can securely transfer partially-signed transactions between hardware wallets and coordinators. We also bump the safety by automatically verifying output addresses - including change - while displaying all of this to the user to verify as well. Want MultiSig Bliss? Pre-order today!

What do we do to make this project independently verifiable?

We are committed to open source (MIT License) of the whole project. Firmware, hardware schematics, parts list, etc. Using docker, our goal is to always release reproducible builds. We will also reach out to members of the community to build it themselves and sign the build with their PGP keys, allowing our users to verify their software from many sources. The community will be free to build this on their own, however we recognize its much easier to buy it from us than to start soldering boards. That's where we come in! Pre-order today!
